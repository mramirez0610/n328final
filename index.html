Comparing Gas Prices

   For my data visualization project, I wasn't quite sure what to do. I searched around for a few days, and couldn't find a dataset that I as truly passionate about. I continued to think at work, as I drove, and as I existed. Then, the idea struck me. As I filled my car's gas tank, I started to think about two years ago, when gas was cheap. I then started to ponder how cheap it was 10 years ago, and then 20. Finally, I had an idea. Using D3.js, I decidede to visualize how gas prices have changed through the years, as well as comparing the prices per gallon of "All Grades" (regular, medium, premium), as well as the prices of "Diesel".
